Travel Tips

  1. Always keep digital copies of your important documents.
  2. Learn a few basic phrases in the local language.
  3. Carry a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated.
  4. Use public transport — it’s cheaper and often faster than taxis.
  5. Don’t overpack — less luggage = more freedom.
