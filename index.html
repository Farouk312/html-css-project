Introduction

Welcome to my travel log. This is the unvarnished truth of the journey—the good, the bad, the breathtaking, and the mildly disastrous. Inside these pages, you’ll find itineraries, expenses, must-see spots, and a few hidden gems I was lucky enough to discover. It’s a practical guide for my future self and for anyone else who might follow in these footsteps. Here's to getting lost, finding our way, and collecting stories to tell.
